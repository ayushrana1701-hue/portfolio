About Me

I’m Yashpal Singh Belwal, a third-year B.Sc. Animation and Gaming student at Graphic Era Hill University, hailing from the beautiful state of Uttarakhand, India. Born and raised in Dehradun, the “Land of Gods,” I’ve always been inspired by the culture, natural beauty, and stories that surround me. My passion lies in animation, 3D modeling, and visual storytelling — bringing characters and worlds to life through design and motion. Over the course of my studies, I’ve developed skills in 3D modeling, texturing, lighting, rigging, animation, game design, and VFX, working with tools like Maya, Blender, ZBrush, and Unreal Engine. I’m particularly fascinated by the creative and technical challenges of character modeling and environment design, and I aim to craft immersive experiences for film, games, and interactive media. With dedication, curiosity, and continuous learning, I aspire to contribute to projects that inspire, entertain, and connect with audiences worldwide.
